Our Mission

Our goal is to represent the java joint, a cafe providing locally sourced organic food such as coffees, pastries, and other Italian delicacie.

Who we are?

The audience for our website is any potential customers who would like to experience the height of dining in the 21st century. That is, people who appreciate knowing about their food, who made it, where it came from, and what is in it prior to consumption. the java joint is interested in branding itself as a trendy establishment that cares just as much about its customers as it does about preparing great foods. The website will therefore target audiences that particularly resonate with the java jointâ€™s values, namely people who are ages 18 to 35 looking for somewhere that provides them with healthy food and thus, a healthy conscience. Our users will have the motivation to learn more about the java joint and discover the foods they can expect to eat there. Some users may also like to know about the nutritional value and dietary information of menu items. Therefore, our audience will be able to see the allergic information of food items in order to choose their food so that it is in line with their requirements. Our users will also have the motivation to read and know the process which is involved in the preparation of a particular dish. The tasks that our users would like to conduct on our website include learning more about the java joint, especially through understanding its mission and whether the cafe is ethically sourcing its ingredients. They would like to know the story behind the cafe, its sustainable practices, and how it is giving back to the community.
